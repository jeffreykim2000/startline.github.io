Plan and Pricing

Startline provides multiple plans to suit you and your business’s need. All plans are charged monthly with no contracts, no setup fees, and no hidden gimmicks.



You are free to upgrade or downgrade to any plan anytime. Plan changes will be made effective after the next billing date

We offer a monthly subscription service with no long-term contracts.
Users are billed monthly for the upcoming month's service. The subscription can be terminated at any time before the next billing date without any penalties or refunds.

Here's how our termination policy works:

  1. Payment Cycle: When a user signs up for our service, they are billed for the upcoming month. The subscription plan runs from the day of payment until the day before the next payment date.

  2. Refunds: Once a payment date has passed, refunds are not provided for that billing cycle. Users are responsible for the full monthly fee if they cancel after the payment date has passed.

  3. Termination: Upon cancellation, the subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle and automatic renewal will stop. Users will not be billed for the subsequent month.

Setting up an account

Once you have signed up for Startline, you should have already chosen whether you are a startup or corporate user. If you have not done that yet, check out here and make sure you sign up!


Did you know that you need to verify your account before being able to create your company card?

To find out how to verify your account, check out here and prepare the necessary documents before you proceed!


Startup users will be directed to the startup profile page where we require you to fill out your information.

The information you provide will be used to create your own company card on our platform.






CEO Contact
This section facilitates direct communication with your CEO, allowing key clients to engage with your company's leadership for valuable business opportunities.

Basic Information:
Comprising key details such as contact information, website URL, company size, founding year, industry sector, headquarters location, and descriptive tags, this section offers a comprehensive overview of your organization.

Team Information:
Enable potential customers to connect effortlessly by showcasing your dedicated team members and their roles within your company.

Product List:
Presenting detailed product information such as product photos, brochures, names, and descriptions, this section offers easy access to the array of offerings provided to your customers.

Financials: By detailing your funding stage, this section provides clients with insights into your company's stability and growth trajectory, fostering confidence in your business.

To know more about the different funding rounds that you can choose from, check out the information here!

After entering your information, click on "Save" to store the inputted data. Subsequently, proceed to "Verify Account" to confirm your association with the stated company, ensuring authentication and establishing credibility.































Upon verification, you gain the ability to craft a distinctive company card on our search page. This card serves as a gateway for potential customers to explore and familiarize themselves with your business, enhancing visibility and facilitating informed decision-making.


Corporate users will be directed to the corporate profile page where we require you to fill out your information.

The information you provide will be used to create your own company card on our platform.






CEO Contact
This contains information about your CEO so that top clients can reach out directly to your CEO to offer business opportunities!

Basic Information:
This contains information basic information such as contact information, website url, company size, founding year, industry sector, headquarter location and tags that represent your company.

Team Information:
This contains information about your team members so that potential customers can easily reach out to you

Product List:
This contains product information such as product photo, brochure, product name and description about the product so that others can have easy access of the things you provide to your customers

Financials: This contains information about your funding stage so that clients have an idea about the stability and growth size of your company.


Once you have filled in your information, click on save to save the information you have inputed and then verify account to verify that you are really from the company that you state you are from.
