Features


Startline provides various features to improve business efficiency for you and your company. Check out what we have included in our platform make your work life easier and more efficient.


Personalization


Startline allows all users to personalize the platform for their own needs.


By entering answers in the the AI Fine tuning in the my company tab, Startline’s AI will recommend unique companies based on your interest.




By clicking on the like or dislike button, your personalized AI model will be narrowed down so that you are provided with companies according to your interest.



The bookmark feature on the company cards are another way you can personalize your experience.
By bookmarking companies that you are interested in, you can save it in the bookmark setting (Settings -> Bookmark Settings) so that you can utilize the filter to only compare companies that you have bookmarked.









Daily Recommendations


Startline’s AI Model is able to get information in real-time, allowing users to be provided with unique daily recommendations.



Daily recommendations allow users to view companies according to their personalized information to find hidden companies that are within their search needs.



By clicking on the like or dislike button, your personalized AI model will be narrowed down so that you are provided with companies according to your interest.










Search


Startline’s platform provides a search page for all corporate users. Using the search page, users can filter companies based on their needs such as location, finance stage and their bookmark.





After filtering companies that they want to find, they are able to browse through the different company cards to find the company of their need.





By clicking on the “More Details” button in the bottom-right corner of the company card, users are able to view comprehensive information about each company. The content consist of basic information, product list, financials and contact information.
