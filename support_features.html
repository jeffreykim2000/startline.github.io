Personalization

You are able to select whether you want to be a startup or corporate user of Startline. To know the difference between the two, check out here.



Startup users will be directed to the startup profile page where we require you to fill out your:


Basic Information:
This contains information basic information such as contact information, website url, company size, founding year, industry sector, headquarter location and tags that represent your company.

Team Information:
This contains information about your team members so that potential customers can easily reach out to you

Product List:
This contains product information such as product photo, brochure, product name and description about the product so that others can have easy access of the things you provide to your customers

Financials: This contains information about your funding stage so that clients have an idea about the stability and growth size of your company.

Once you have filled in your information, click on save to save the information you have inputed and then verify account to verify that you are really from the company that you state you are from.



























And thats it! Once you are verified you will be able to create your own company card on our search page so that potential customers of your business can search your company and learn about your company!

Corporate users will be directed to the corporate profile page where we require you to fill out your:


Basic Information:
This contains basic information such as contact information of the person in charge, industry sector you belong and the location of your headquarter

Team Member Invitation:
In this section, input your team member’s email and click send invitation to invite your co-worker onto the Startline workspace under your team.

Search Customization:
This contains some questions we have for you so that we can better manage what kind of companies we should recommend to you!















And thats it! Once you have completed up till here, you are good to go to start searching!
