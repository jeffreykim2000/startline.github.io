Create an account

You can create a free account anytime without choosing a paid plan. With this account, you can try out the different features that Startline offers and if we meet your needs, try upgrading!




If you are hoping to join a team, use the link that was emailed to you from the team admin. Do not create a new account from the registration page. One account can only belong to one team.




To start, head to our Sign Up page. Create an account with your work email and new password with at least 12 characters.




After creating an account, select the plan that you would like to use




And that’s it! Simple and easy. You have successfully created a new account on Startline. Next up, you should set up your company profile!
